WENDY'S SPIDER SOLITAIRE

SCORE: 500
MOVES: 0

How to Play

The Goal: Assemble 13 cards of a suit, in descending sequence from King to Ace. Once a full sequence is built, it will automatically move to the foundations. Build 8 foundations to win!

General Rules

  • You can move a single card to another pile if it is exactly one rank lower than the card it lands on (e.g., place a 7 on an 8).
  • You can move a stack of cards only if they are of the same suit and in proper descending order.
  • Any face-up card or valid sequence can be moved into an empty column.

Game Modes (Configurable in Settings ⚙️)

👑 Wendy's Rules (Default)

A relaxed, modern way to play! You may tap the Stock (top left) to deal a new row of cards at any time, regardless of the board state. This keeps the game flowing and fun.

⚖️ Original Rules

The strict, traditional challenge. You cannot deal a new row of cards from the Stock if you have any completely empty columns on the board. You must fill all spaces first!

Settings

Wendy's: Deal anytime.
Original: Must fill empty slots to deal.
